The Unmistakable Pattern

When examined individually, each prophecy provides compelling evidence. But when viewed together, they form an undeniable pattern that could only point to one person: Muhammad ibn Abdullah, the final messenger of God (peace be upon him). From his lineage as an Ishmaelite, to his mission to both Jews and Gentiles, to his bringing of a new revelation in a foreign language (Arabic), to his physical description, name, and seal of prophethood—every detail was foretold centuries before his birth.

The Quran confirms this truth: "Those who follow the Messenger, the unlettered prophet, whom they find written in what they have of the Torah and the Gospel..." (Quran 7:157). The evidence is clear for those who seek with an open heart and mind.
